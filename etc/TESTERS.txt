To build application: mvn compile exec:java
To view application: localhost:4567

Instructions:
To challenge a player, you will need two browsers, each logged on.
Each player will appear as a button.
Click the button to start up a game.

Completed and Testable:
Player Sign-in
Player Sign-out
Starting a game
Ending a game by logging out

In Development and Not Testable:
Moving Pieces
Manual Resignation
Automatic Resignation (Lose conditions)
